# RED SEA LION — ROLE LIFECYCLE MAPS

This document traces the 13 canonical stages for all 12 project-scoped roles (`executive`, `production`, `management`, `crew`, `staff`, `talent`, `vendor`, `client`, `sponsor`, `press`, `guest`, `attendee`).

## 1. Governance Roles: Executive, Production, Management
*These roles control the production lifecycle and dictate upstream approvals.*

1. **Discovery**: Internal assignment via Platform Org hierarchy.
2. **Qualification**: Internal verification (Title/Dept assignment).
3. **Onboarding**: Provisioned via ATLVS SSO; assigned `role_prj_exec`/`role_prj_prod`.
4. **Contracting**: Execution of master service agreements (if agency).
5. **Scheduling**: Global master schedule visibility; high-level run of show (ROS).
6. **Advancing**: Final sign-off authority on technical riders and advances.
7. **Deployment**: All-access credential map; arrival via executive manifest.
8. **Active Operations**: Issue logging, change order approval, override authority.
9. **Demobilization**: Sign-off on strike; closure of venue.
10. **Settlement**: Approval of vendor POs and crew timesheets.
11. **Reconciliation**: Budget vs. actual sign-off.
12. **Archival**: Performance review of crew/vendors.
13. **Closeout**: Project status set to CLOSED; logs locked.

## 2. Fulfillment Roles: Crew, Staff
*These roles operate heavily within COMPVSS and focus on logistics and operations execution.*

1. **Discovery**: Sourced via UAC/TPC crew roster or inbound referral.
2. **Qualification**: W9, COI (if required), ID upload, union card check.
3. **Onboarding**: COMPVSS app registration, direct deposit setup.
4. **Contracting**: E-sign Deal Memo or Short Form Agreement.
5. **Scheduling**: Shift assignment via Master Schedule (with RFC5545 RRULE).
6. **Advancing**: Travel/Lodging/Per Diem confirmation.
7. **Deployment**: Geo-fenced check-in via COMPVSS mobile.
8. **Active Operations**: Task execution, incident reporting, break logging.
9. **Demobilization**: Shift checkout, equipment return.
10. **Settlement**: Timesheet submission vs. Deal Memo matching.
11. **Reconciliation**: 1099/W2 export staging.
12. **Archival**: Crew rating (1-5 stars) and strike from active roster.
13. **Closeout**: Read-only access to past earning statements.

## 3. Supply/Asset Roles: Vendor
*These roles own WHAT/WHERE entities and interact via B2B interfaces.*

1. **Discovery**: Sourced from UAC.
2. **Qualification**: COI, W9, MSA verification.
3. **Onboarding**: Portal creation for agency/org.
4. **Contracting**: SOW execution, line-item negotiation.
5. **Scheduling**: Load-in/Load-out slot assignment.
6. **Advancing**: Technical advance, logistics approval, power/internet drops.
7. **Deployment**: BOL (Bill of Lading) reconciliation on dock.
8. **Active Operations**: Asset tracking, maintenance, change orders.
9. **Demobilization**: Return logistics, asset damage assessment (flags).
10. **Settlement**: Invoice matching via PO and Net-30 payment processing.
11. **Reconciliation**: Tax entity reporting.
12. **Archival**: Vendor scorecard and SLA review.
13. **Closeout**: Final ledger entry.

## 4. Key Figure Roles: Talent, Client, Sponsor
*These roles represent high-value stakeholders requiring VIP/white-glove lifecycles.*

1. **Discovery**: Agency pitch / Sales pipeline hand-in.
2. **Qualification**: Contract vetting.
3. **Onboarding**: Concierge portal access via GVTEWAY.
4. **Contracting**: Long-form Performance Agreement or Sponsorship deck.
5. **Scheduling**: Call times, green room availability.
6. **Advancing**: Hospitality rider fulfillment (catering, specific lodging).
7. **Deployment**: Escorted access, VIP credentialing.
8. **Active Operations**: Green room status, stage management cues.
9. **Demobilization**: Departure logistics, private transit.
10. **Settlement**: Performance fees, backend cuts.
11. **Reconciliation**: Media/activation deliverable proofs.
12. **Archival**: Retention of signed releases and media assets.
13. **Closeout**: Relationship handover back to Sales/Management.

## 5. Consumer/External Roles: Press, Guest, Attendee
*These roles are bulk-processed, high-volume participants governed by GVTEWAY.*

1. **Discovery**: Ticketing platforms, RSVP forms, press accreditation requests.
2. **Qualification**: Auto-validation for attendees; Editor assignment for press.
3. **Onboarding**: Digital ticket/QR issuance.
4. **Contracting**: Terms & Conditions / Liability Waiver check-box.
5. **Scheduling**: Event start time / gate open notifications.
6. **Advancing**: Parking, restricted item lists (Clear Bag Policy).
7. **Deployment**: Entry scan, credential issuance (lanyard/wristband).
8. **Active Operations**: Zone access control, point-of-sale limits.
9. **Demobilization**: Egress routing.
10. **Settlement**: Merch/F&B tab closure.
11. **Reconciliation**: Total scan vs. capacity metrics.
12. **Archival**: CRM add/update for marketing.
13. **Closeout**: Post-event survey distribution.
